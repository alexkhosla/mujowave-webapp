Privacy Policy for Mujowave

Effective date: 5 July 2026

Mujowave's Android sailing instrument app accesses your phone's GPS, Bluetooth, sensors, and battery information to display sailing metrics.

Information accessed

The app may access location data, including GPS position and speed over ground; Bluetooth connectivity data; device sensors such as the accelerometer, gyroscope, and compass; and battery status.

How it is used

This information is used only to provide Mujowave's sailing instrument features on your device. Mujowave has no user accounts, includes no advertising, and does not sell or monetise personal information.

Data sharing

Mujowave does not transmit your GPS, Bluetooth, sensor, or battery data to the developer or to a cloud service. Local Bluetooth exchanges with compatible hardware occur only as needed for functionality.

Retention

Mujowave does not store long-term sailing history on a server. Session data is temporary and used only during active app operation.

Permissions

The app requests location and Bluetooth permissions to enable its core sailing instrument and hardware connectivity features.
